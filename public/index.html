What is WOOY

At Wooy, we work with NGOs to reward and engage donors by gifting them unique NFT PODs (Poof Of Donations).

What are PODs?

PODs are non-fungible tokens that represent proofs of donations made to the NGOs we work with.

Each POD is unique and represents a specific donation made by a donor.
